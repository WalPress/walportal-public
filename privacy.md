# WalPortal Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: September 11, 2025**

## Introduction

WalPortal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the WalPortal browser extension (the "Extension"). By installing and using WalPortal, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Automatically Collected Information

**Browsing Data:**
- Walrus site URLs you visit (walrus:// and wal:// protocols only)
- Site visit timestamps
- Site verification status
- Extension usage patterns

**Technical Information:**
- Browser type and version
- Extension version
- Network settings (mainnet/testnet selection)
- RPC endpoint configurations
- Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

**Analytics Data (Optional):**
- If you opt-in, we may collect anonymous usage analytics through Amplitude
- This includes feature usage, error reports, and performance metrics
- No personally identifiable information is included in analytics

### 2. Information You Provide

**Settings and Preferences:**
- Custom RPC URLs you configure
- Security preferences (blocking unverified sites)
- Network selection (mainnet/testnet)
- Extension activation status

**No Personal Information:**
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
- We do not access your regular web browsing history (only Walrus sites)
- We do not collect wallet addresses or private keys

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. Core Functionality
- **Site Resolution:** Process walrus:// and wal:// URLs to display Walrus sites
- **Site Verification:** Check site verification status to enhance security
- **History Management:** Store recent Walrus site visits for easy access
- **Preference Storage:** Remember your settings and configurations

### 2. Service Improvement
- **Performance Optimization:** Analyze usage patterns to improve loading times
- **Feature Development:** Understand which features are most valuable to users
- **Bug Detection:** Identify and fix technical issues

### 3. Security and Safety
- **Malicious Site Detection:** Monitor for potentially harmful Walrus sites
- **Verification System:** Maintain database of verified sites
- **Security Warnings:** Alert users to potential risks

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 1. Third-Party Services

**Walrus Network:**
- Your requests to access Walrus sites are sent to Walrus aggregators
- This is necessary for core functionality and occurs only when you visit Walrus sites

**SuiNS Resolution:**
- Domain name lookups are sent to Sui RPC nodes for resolution
- Required for translating human-readable names to object IDs

**Analytics Provider (Amplitude):**
- If opted-in, anonymous usage data is shared with Amplitude
- No personally identifiable information is included
- Data is aggregated and anonymized

### 2. We Do Not Sell Your Data
- We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information
- We do not share your data with advertisers for targeting purposes
- We do not provide your information to data brokers

### 3. Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, such as:
- Responding to valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
- Protecting our rights and safety
- Preventing fraud or abuse

## Data Storage and Security

### 1. Local Storage
- Most data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- This includes browsing history, preferences, and settings
- Data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers

### 2. Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- We implement industry-standard security practices
- Regular security audits and updates
- No sensitive data (like private keys) is ever stored or transmitted

### 3. Data Retention
- **Browsing History:** Stored locally for 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **Settings:** Retained until you uninstall the extension
- **Analytics:** Anonymous data retained for 2 years maximum

## Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### 1. Data Control
- **Clear History:** Delete your Walrus site browsing history at any time
- **Reset Settings:** Clear all stored preferences and configurations
- **Disable Analytics:** Opt-out of usage analytics in extension settings

### 2. Extension Controls
- **Toggle Functionality:** Turn the extension on/off as desired
- **Selective Use:** Choose which Walrus sites to visit
- **Uninstall:** Remove all data by uninstalling the extension

### 3. Transparency
- **Open Source:** Core functionality code may be made available for review
- **Clear Indicators:** Extension clearly shows when it's active
- **No Hidden Features:** All functionality is documented and transparent

## Advertising and Monetization

### 1. Advertisements

- Ad content is contextually relevant to the Walrus ecosystem
- You can provide feedback on ad relevance through extension settings

### 2. No Tracking for Ads
- We do not track your browsing behavior for advertising purposes
- Ads are selected based on general category interest, not personal data
